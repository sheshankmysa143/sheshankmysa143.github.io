Frequently Asked Questions


1. How do I book a movie ticket?

To book a ticket, visit the Movies section, select your movie, and click "Book Now".


2. Do I need to create an account to book tickets?

Yes, you need to log in to book movie tickets. If you don’t have an account, you can sign up for free.


3. Can I cancel or change my booking?

Unfortunately, once a ticket is booked, it cannot be canceled or modified. Please check your details carefully before confirming your booking.


4. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments via credit/debit cards, digital wallets (Google Pay, Apple Pay), and UPI payments.


5. Will I receive a confirmation after booking?

Yes! Once your payment is successful, you will receive an email confirmation with your ticket details.


6. Can I book tickets for someone else?

Yes! You can enter the details of the person attending the movie while booking.


7. Is there a discount for group bookings?

Yes, we offer discounts for group bookings of **10 or more tickets**. Contact our support team for more details.


8. How do I log out of my account?

Click the **"Logout"** button in the top-right corner of the page. This will log you out and redirect you to the login page.


9. What if I forget my password?

Click the **"Forgot Password?"** link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.


10. How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our support team via the **"Contact Us"** page or email us at support@movietime.com.
